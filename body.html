Top Startups in India

Flipkart

Founded in 2007, Flipkart is one of India's leading e-commerce platforms, offering a wide range of products from electronics to fashion.

Ola Cabs

Launched in 2010, Ola is a ride-hailing company that has revolutionized transportation in India with its convenient and affordable services.

Byju's

Byju's, founded in 2011, is a leading ed-tech company that provides online learning programs for students of all ages, from primary school to competitive exams.

Paytm

Paytm, founded in 2010, is a digital payments and financial services platform, offering a wide range of services from mobile recharges to banking.

Swiggy

Founded in 2014, Swiggy is one of India's top food delivery platforms, offering fast and reliable delivery from a vast network of restaurants.
